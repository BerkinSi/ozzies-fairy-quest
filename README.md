# Ozzie's Fairy Quest

A magical fairy adventure game designed by **Ozan** (age 8) and built by Uncle Berkin with Claude.

## Play Now

**[Play Ozzie's Fairy Quest](https://berkinsili.github.io/ozzies-fairy-quest/)**

## About

In this game, you play as a regular fairy navigating through an epic adventure across Fire and Ice Fairy Castles, battling bosses, solving mazes, and collecting magical powers.

Ozan designed the entire game story, characters, and mechanics. His vision includes 15 chapters of fairy adventure - this is the first release covering Chapters 1-3.

### Chapters

- **Chapter 1: The Fire Fairy Castle** - Explore a forest, sneak into the Fire Castle, navigate a maze, and collect fire powers (teleportation, fire arrows, fire sword, fireballs)
- **Chapter 2: The Ice Fairy Castle** - Battle the Fire Fairy boss, earn the transformation necklace, and collect ice powers
- **Chapter 3: The Witch's Tower** - Dodge enchanted brooms, meet the Sun God, defeat the witch, and gain the magical hat and wizard's staff

### Controls

**Desktop:**
- Arrow keys or WASD to move
- Click to shoot (in combat chapters)
- Click to advance dialog

**Mobile/Tablet:**
- On-screen D-pad to move
- Fire button to shoot (auto-aims at enemies)
- Tap to advance dialog

## Tech

Single HTML file using [Phaser 3](https://phaser.io/) game engine. All graphics are drawn with Phaser's built-in shapes - no external assets needed. Works in any modern browser.
